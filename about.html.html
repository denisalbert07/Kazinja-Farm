About Us

Kazinja Farm Limited is committed to raising healthy pigs and providing quality meat products. Our farm focuses on proper feeding, vaccinations, hygiene, and care for all pigs. We teach modern pig farming techniques to ensure sustainability, maximize growth, and maintain health. Visitors and customers are welcome to learn how to raise pigs properly, buy piglets, pigs, and fresh meat. Our farm is located in Pwani, Tanzania.
